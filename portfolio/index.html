论文

  • 基于HTC VIVE的可穿戴上肢康复训练系统的研究
    陈东林，刘昊，任志刚
    北京服装学院学报[中文核心]2018

    基于人体上肢运动学理论，使用D-H参数方法建立人体上肢5自由度模型，仿真模拟了上肢手腕部的运动空间，以确保训练的安全性。以此为基础设计训练方案，并在Unreal Engine 4中构建虚拟康复训练场景。患者通过操纵控制器触碰虚拟场景中的目标点完成训练任务。经上肢行为正常人士及偏瘫病人临床测试验证，训练任务有效促进了病人参与康复训练的主动性，运动轨迹记录可及时反馈患肢的运动情况，系统满足康复训练的需求。

  • 基于Unity3D的RPG游戏开发
    陈东林，刘昊
    2016人工智能与计算机科学国际学术会议

    本文通过一款基于Unity3D的RPG游戏项目，概述RPG游戏的整体设计、任务系统设计、界面系统设计和游戏主场景的开发，介绍了使用NGUI插件搭建UI(User Interface 用户界面)，并通过功能分化为菜单、对话框以及背包系统，解析了任务系统的设计以及游戏角色控制的常用方法。

  • 数字信号处理中的量化效应研究
    陈东林，陈勇
    江西师范大学学士学位论文

    本文从A/ D 变换器、系统参数及运算三个方面进行了量化误差分析。这些量化效应主要和计算中用的寄存器长度有关，寄存器长度愈长，量化效应愈小。

Papers

  • Application of Wearable Device HTC VIVE in Upper Rehabilitation Training
    Donglin Chen,Hao Liu,Zhigang Ren
    IEEE IMCEC 2018

    By analyzing the human upper limb kinematics with Denavit- Hartenberg parameter method, it has established a 5-DOF model of human upper limb , and the work area of the upper limbs wrist was simulated and analyzed to ensure the training safety. The user study shows the system has promoted the patient's initiative for participating in rehabilitation training, the motion track records the movement of the limbs, and it meets the needs of rehabilitation training.

  • RPG Game Development Based On Unity3D
    Donglin Chen,Hao Liu
    AICS2016

    This paper through a RPG game project which based on Unity3D platform,Overview the overall design,the design of the task system,the interface system design and the development of the main scene for the RPG, it introduced the use of NGUI plug-in to build UI(User Interface)，and the function is divided into the menu, dialog box and the backpack system,The design of task system and the common methods of game role control are analyzed.

    Donglin Chen,Yong Chen
    Jiangxi Normal University Bachelor Paper
